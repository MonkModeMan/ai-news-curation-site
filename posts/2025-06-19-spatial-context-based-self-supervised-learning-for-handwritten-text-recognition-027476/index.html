Computer Science > Artificial Intelligence [Submitted on 17 Apr 2024 (v1), last revised 18 Jun 2025 (this version, v3)] Title:Spatial Context-based Self-Supervised Learning for Handwritten Text Recognition View PDF HTML (experimental)Abstract:Handwritten Text Recognition (HTR) is a relevant problem in computer vision, and implies unique challenges owing to its inherent variability and the rich contextualization required for its interpretation. Despite the success of Self-Supervised Learning (SSL) in computer vision, its application to HTR has been rather scattered, leaving key SSL methodologies unexplored. This work focuses on one of them, namely Spatial Context-based SSL. We investigate how this family of approaches can be adapted and optimized for HTR and propose new workflows that leverage the unique features of handwritten text. Our experiments demonstrate that the methods considered lead to advancements in the state-of-the-art of SSL for HTR in a number of benchmark cases.
